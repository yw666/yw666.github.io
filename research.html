Research Areas

What we study

Core research pillars driving the Edge Camouflage agenda at MIT and beyond.

01
Evasion Attacks on Vision Models
Imperceptible perturbations that fool classifiers, detectors, and segmentation models under white-box and black-box constraints.
02
Physical-World Adversarial Patches
Printed adversarial patches that persist under real-world conditions: lighting variation, viewpoint changes, and compression.
03
LLM Red-Teaming & Jailbreaking
Automated adversarial prompt generation to expose safety misalignments in instruction-tuned language models at scale.
04
Transferability & Black-Box Attacks
Exploiting cross-model transferability to enable attacks without direct model access.
05
Certified Robustness & Defenses
Provably robust defenses via randomized smoothing, adversarial training, and formal verification methods.
06
Multimodal & Cross-Modal Attacks
Adversarial attacks on vision-language systems using synchronized perturbations across image and text modalities.
