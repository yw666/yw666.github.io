Research Areas

What we study

Six core pillars driving the Edge Camouflage research agenda.

01
Evasion Attacks on Vision Models
Generating imperceptible perturbations that fool state-of-the-art image classifiers, detectors, and segmentation models under white-box and black-box constraints.
02
Physical-World Adversarial Patches
Designing printed adversarial textures that persist under real-world conditions: lighting variation, viewpoint changes, and compression.
03
LLM Red-Teaming & Jailbreaking
Automated adversarial prompt generation exposing safety misalignments in instruction-tuned language models at scale.
04
Transferability & Black-Box Attacks
Exploiting cross-model transferability to enable potent attacks without direct model access or gradient information.
05
Certified Robustness & Defenses
Provably robust defenses using randomized smoothing, adversarial training, and formal verification methods.
06
Multimodal & Cross-Modal Attacks
Synchronized adversarial perturbations across image and text modalities targeting vision-language models.
